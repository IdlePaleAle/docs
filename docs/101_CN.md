## 团队为什么要匿名？

总而言之: **去中心化**

- Don't lean on founder reputations不依赖创始人的名誉
- Avoid "cult leader" vulnerability避免形成盲目崇拜
- Foster grassroots organic community促进有机的草根社区
- Empower project to stand on its own使得项目可以独立
- Minimize chance of being shut down最小化被封杀的可能性
- Reduce social exploit attack surface降低被社交攻击的可能性
- Focus on the product and ecosystem专注于产品和生态
