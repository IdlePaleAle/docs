### How does it work?

Just go to [earn.octo.fi](https://earn.octo.fi) and deposit $DAI, $USDC, $USDT, $LEND, $COMP, and/or $OCTO to start earning interest on your tokens.

You'll need to have $OCTO tokens in your connected wallet for access.
